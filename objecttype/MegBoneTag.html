MegBoneTag

Prefix:megbonetag@Identity Format:Identified using the format megbonetag@<entity>,<model_blueprint>,<bone_id>.Description:Represents a bone on a model.View source

Tags​

Tag: MegBoneTag.children​

Returns:MapTag(MegBoneTag)Description:Returns a map of all the children of the bone, with the bone id as the key and the bone as the value.

Tag: MegBoneTag.damage_tint​

Returns:ColorTagMechanism:damage_tintDescription:Returns the damage tint of the bone.

Tag: MegBoneTag.default_tint​

Returns:ColorTagMechanism:damage_tintDescription:Returns the default tint of the bone.

Tag: MegBoneTag.id​

Returns:ElementTagDescription:Returns the id of the bone.

Tag: MegBoneTag.item​

Returns:ItemTagMechanism:itemDescription:Returns the underlying item that the bone uses.

Tag: MegBoneTag.location​

Returns:LocationTagDescription:Returns the location of the bone.

Tag: MegBoneTag.skin_texture​

Returns:ElementTagMehcanism:skin_textureDescription:Returns the skin texture of the bone. Returns null if the bone doesn't have a skin texture. The format is UUID|Texture|Name.

Mechanisms​

Mech: MegBoneTag.damage_tint​

Input:ColorTagDescription:Sets the damage tint of the bone.Tags:MegBoneTag.damage_tint

Mech: MegBoneTag.default_tint​

Input:ColorTagDescription:Sets the default tint of the bone.Tags:MegBoneTag.default_tint

Mech: MegBoneTag.item​

Input:ItemTagDescription:Sets the underlying item that the bone uses. Shouldn't be used unless you know what you're doing.Tags:MegBoneTag.item

Mech: MegBoneTag.skin_texture​

Input:ElementTagDescription:Sets the skin texture of the bone. The format is UUID|Texture|Name.Tags:MegBoneTag.skin_texture
